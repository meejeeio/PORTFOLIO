About Me

Clinician, coder,
Researcher

I grew up in a remote village in Sindhupalchowk with limited access to education and medical care. Walking three hours to school daily, facing financial hardship — none of it dimmed the drive. A full scholarship in biology led to another at Maharajgunj Medical Campus under IOM.

Today I sit at the intersection of medicine and technology, building tools that bring healthcare closer to those who need it most — and learning something new every single day.
